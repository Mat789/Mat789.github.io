Welcome

The Workshop on Decentralized Control and Reinforcement Learning (DCRL 2025) brings together researchers and practitioners to discuss advances at the intersection of distributed control systems and reinforcement learning techniques.

Scope & Topics

  • Decentralized and distributed control architectures
  • Learning-based adaptive control
  • Multi-agent reinforcement learning
  • Applications in robotics, networks, and power systems
